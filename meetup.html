RU Tech Meetup

September 13th, 2013 - Presented by USACS, RUMAD, Microsoft, and IEEE

Rutgers Tech Meetup is a chance for the programming student body of Rutgers to come together and demo the awesome projects that they've been working on over the summer. Come see what open source projects, production ready apps, and side-project hacks your fellow students have built in their free time over the summer. We'll also be serving food and some awesome guest speaker talks.

RU Tech meetup will be held 6pm in The Cove at the Busch Campus Center. Get your ticket now!

Guest Speakers

  • Mike Swift, founder of HackerLeague and former Evangelist at SendGrid: "On community"
  • Pete Sullivan, former CEO of Tripl: "Life after failing"
  • George Matthews, Project Manager at Microsoft
