Becoming a member of USACS

Being apart of USACS means being connected with the Rutgers Computer Science Community. With events ranging from our annual semester Hackathon, weekly Hacker Hours, to the CS Trivia Nights, USACS takes charge of the largest CS events Rutgers has to offer!

Such a diverse community is one that prospers with growth, and with growth comes new members. If you are someone who is intrigued by technology, coding, social outings, teaching, and or leadership, checkout out our full events calendar to get involved!
