    • Schedule for Spring 2013 Semester

    • Hacker Hour

      Hacker Hour will be being held on Friday nights at 6pm in Hill 252 (the CAVE). Here's our upcoming Hacker Hour Schedule.

      • Friday, Feburary 22nd - Getting a Job and Killing the Technical Interview

        Come learn how to land an interview to ace the technical questions for your next job or internship. We'll talk about building an online presence, writing a baller resume, and strategy on how to take on the hardest technical interview questions.

      • Friday, March 1st and 8th - Game Development in HTML5 and Javascript

        This will be a two part series on how to get started in writing games on the web. But not the derpy flash games that you grew up on. This will be lean, mean, HTML5 Javascript games that can be played better, faster, and on any browser.

    • HackRU

      USACS will be holding HackRU once more this spring. Expect an over-night programming challenge of epic proportions. With help of local tech-company sponsors we will be eating, hacking, and competing for 24-hours in the Cook Campus Center on March 30th to 31st. Come compete, win prizes, make job connections, build something awesome, and have fun! Details are on HackRU.org

    • Rutgers Tech Meetup

      This semester we will be hosting the first ever Rutgers Tech Meetup. Come and share some of your awesome work with the community, see others demo their work, eat burritos and have fun. Details to follow in the next few weeks.
