Upcoming Events

  • USACS + IEEE Robotics Competition

    Saturday November 22nd 2014 - 11:30am to 10:00pm @ Livingston Student Center Hall B
    Come join USACS and the IEEE for a day of robotics madness. You and your team will learn how to program a robot to undertake exciting challenges
    in our Maze Of Doom. No prior robotics experience necessary.

  • Hack Night

    Mondays November 10th and 24th - 8pm @ The Cave (Hill 252)
    Come and work on your projects with others, or search for a team who needs more people. Or get ideas for a project if you don't already have one! Pizza and soda will be served.

  • RU Tech Meetup

    Friday February 28th - 6pm @ Center Hall (BCC)
    Come check out what other students have been working on at Rutgers as well as tech demos from prominent companies. Food will be served.

  • HackRU

    Saturday October 11th to Sunday April 12th @ Rutgers Student Center
    Come join hackers from around the country in a 24 hour hackathon for money and prizes! More information at hackru.org.
